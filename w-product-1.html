Developed by Aviron’s Scientific Department in collaboration with professional athletes, the Tough Series Rower is a full commercial rowing machine designed to survive the usage demands of fitness facilities.

VIBRANT 21.5” HD TOUCHSCREEN

More than just a fancy high definition touchscreen, it is integrated with an innovative resistance system, performance metric sensors, and is connected to Aviron’s network so you can participate in challenges with others across the world.

INNOVATIVE DUAL AIR
& MAGNETIC RESISTANCE SYSTEM

The gradual resistance delivered by the air system mimics the feeling of rowing through water, while the resistance delivered by the magnetic system provides exceptionally strong and smooth torque that can be adjusted on the fly.

EASY RESISTANCE ADJUSTMENT

The dual resistance system can be easily adjusted during a challenge by using the touchscreen or external buttons to help you increase output and close the gap or extend your lead.

INDUSTRIAL POLY-V BELT

The industrial strength belt requires no ongoing maintenance and operates quieter than traditional chain belts.

OTHER INNOVATIVE FEATURES

PREMIUM CONSTRUCTION

The rower including its components have undergone over 1,000,000 testing cycles at peak force load to ensure maximum durability and reliability well beyond normal standards. The oversized anodized aluminum rail delivers high strength and stability.

EASY TRANSPORTATION

2 wheels located at the front make for transportation easy (not foldable)

ERGONOMIC SEAT

Specially designed ergonomic seat with additional rear support make workout sessions more comfortable

INNOVATIVE ADJUSTABLE FOOT PAD

4-way adjustable foot pads allow you to adjust feet length and distance between your feet

LOW FRAME HEIGHT

The low frame height of only Xcm makes getting on and off the rower easy and safe

IMMERSIVE SOUND

Two speakers pump out tunes and sound effects to make your immersive experience even more realistic

SWEAT PROOF

Don’t worry about drenching the equipment and touchscreen in sweat, they’re sweat proof
