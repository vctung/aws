CONNECTED. IMMERSIVE. EXHILERATING

All Aviron equipment have a 21.5” HD touchscreen that integrates with performance metric sensors and an innovative resistance system for a fully immersive experience. The equipment can operate offline but once it is connected to Aviron’s online network via WiFi or Ethernet, it searches the world for teammates and places you in exciting group workouts. You’ll enjoy working out more and be motivated to push yourself further.

THRILLING CHALLENGES

Research shows that competition increases workout attendance by 90% and improves workout performance by 107%. Challenge friends or use Aviron’s application to find others looking for competition. You can even go toe-to-toe with Aviron’s advanced artificial intelligence system designed to push your limits. Aviron’s thrilling challenges are designed for individuals and groups of up to 10 people.

COMPLETE VISABILITY

See a complete history of your workouts including your metrics, achievements and rewards so you can keep track of your success. Show off your performance on social media.

INNOVATIVE RESISTANCE SYSTEM

Adjusting the innovative resistance system will change the difficulty of your workout. A higher resistance will increase your level of difficulty and output, help you close your gap, or extend your lead. Adjust resistance using the touchscreen or external buttons.
